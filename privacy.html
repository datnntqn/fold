Privacy Policy

Effective 14 June 2026

The short version

Fold keeps everything about your wardrobe on your device. There is no account to create, nothing is uploaded to our servers, and we never sell your data. We have no way to see your clothes, your looks, or your plans.

What stays on your device

Everything you add to Fold lives only on your iPhone:

  • Photos you take of your clothes and the cut-outs Fold makes from them
  • Items, looks, and outfit plans you create
  • Notes, costs, and wear counts you record
  • App settings and preferences

If you have iCloud Backup turned on, this data may be included in your own encrypted Apple backup, under your control. We never receive it.

No network for your wardrobe

Fold removes the background from your garment photos using a model that runs entirely on your device. Your images are never sent to us or to any third party for processing.

If you use weather-based suggestions, Fold asks Apple's Weather service for the forecast at your location through your device. That request is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy terms; we do not receive your location or build any profile from it.

No tracking, no ads, no profiling

Fold contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. We do not profile you and there is no feed. Suggestions are produced locally from your own wardrobe and the weather.

Children

Fold is a general-audience app and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it collects nothing.
